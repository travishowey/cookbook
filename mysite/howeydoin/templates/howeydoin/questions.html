This whole project was a collaborative effort. The recipes themselves came from recipes that have been passed down going back to at least my great-grandmother, Leona Roundy. These recipes were then all compiled by Emily Howey, and then illustrated by Liz Howey. The book was then printed and handed off to me, Travis Howey, where I got everything together and created this website

My favorite recipe included in this site is my father's Chili Verde. I don't know how the man did it, but I truly believe that he has perfected the concept of Chili Verde. I eat bowls and bowls of this alone. Top it off with chips and sour cream, and you have one heck of a soup. Find that recipe here
